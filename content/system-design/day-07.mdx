## The Illusion of Competence

Reading a technical article makes you *feel* like you learned something. But if you don't actively retrieve that information from your brain, you will forget it within 48 hours. This is called the "Illusion of Competence."

To actually build strong neural pathways, you must practice **Active Recall**.
