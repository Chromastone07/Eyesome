# Weekly Checkpoint

Congratulations on finishing Week 1 of the DSA Patterns track!

So far, you have learned the 6 most common fundamental patterns for solving array and linked list problems. Instead of memorizing 100 different Leetcode problems, you now have a mental framework to categorize problems the second you read them.

Before we move on to Week 2, it is time to practice **Active Recall**.

## The Forgetting Curve

If you don't actively try to retrieve information from your brain, you will forget roughly 70% of what you read within 24 hours. Reading makes you feel smart; recalling makes you actually smart.
